Custom Mappable Keyboard

  • V
    C7

Why?

Online pianos and programs tend to use this configuration when mapping a piano to a keyboard.

This leads to users using this hand position to "play" the piano, which is uncomfortable and not at all the hand position a piano player would use. (When piano keys are as long as they are, there is no need to force your thumb to be at the same distance from your wrist as your middle finger!)

So just remap the keys.
